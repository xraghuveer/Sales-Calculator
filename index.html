💡 How to Sell More & Fix Low Profit

  • Sell in Bundles: If it's ₱50 each, offer 2 for ₱90 to increase volume.
  • Set a Minimum Order: e.g., "Minimum order ₱100" avoids time-wasting small orders.
  • The "Free Meetup" Trick: Offer free meetup for ₱300+ orders to push buyers to add one more item.
  • Separate Shipping: Never include shipping costs in cheap item prices; charge it separately or via meetup fee.
